RULES

TOURNAMENT RULES:
1. It is an individual event.
2. Registration and payment must be completed online via the link.
3. Participants must be present in the room while playing.
4. Participants will be provided PCs and controllers. The participants can also bring their own controllers or use the keyboard as per their wish. It is the responsibility of all Players to be familiar with the controller’s functions and mode of operation.
5. You CANNOT pause the game unless the game is stopped i.e. if it’s a throw in or ball is in your own keeper’s possession. If you pause the game intentionally, you may be warned or disqualified at referee’s discretion. Second warning will disqualify you automatically. You have 40 seconds after the pause.
6. The game will begin only after the referee confirms all settings are according to the rules.
7. In case of any kind of unfortunate event or disconnection, disc error or electricity failure, the score will remain the same regardless of game played but the half in which the problem occurred will be played again.
8. If the regular time results end in a draw, then the winner will be decided through penalties without extra time.
9. Misconduct during the tournament will disqualify the entire team.
10. Players must refrain from using offensive language.
11. Organizer’s decision will be final under any circumstances.
12. Misconduct during the tournament will disqualify the entire team.
IN - GAME RULES:
 1. Half-time of 4 minutes with difficulty level of AI controlled players at 'Legendary'
2. You can use and customize any tactics but only at the beginning of the game.
3. All matches will be CLASSIC kick-off matches only.
4. NO Timed Shot with all other controls at 'Semi-Assisted'.
5. Tactical defending mode only.
6. You CANNOT select any fictional or All-Star teams. Any regular season teams, as installed with the original game, can be selected. 7. You are only allowed to choose a CLUB team against another CLUB team and an INTERNATIONAL team against an INTERNATIONAL team.
8. Choice is provided to winner of TOSS
9. Both Players CAN select the same team.
10. At the end of regular time, if the match ends in a draw, the winner will be decided through penalties without proceeding to EXTRA TIME.
11. Selecting Preset formations (4-3-3 or 4-4-2 etc) are allowed before the match starts.
12. Once the match has started, players can pause the game to change strategy, tactics and/or formation ONLY when (i)The ball is out of play or during setpieces. (But formation change is not allowed) (ii) a player of his team is sent off (iii) a player of his team is injured. 15. Set pieces are available. Man Marking and Injuries are allowed. 16. No handball
