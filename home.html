About Us

Recharge, the signature cultural fest of REC is all set to charge every performing aspirant out there with a two day cultural extravaganza on the 8th & 9th of March 2020. This edition of Recharge would have 22 plus onstage and offstage events including Battle of Bands, Dance, Adzap and a probable footfall of over 15000 students.
Recharge '20 would also play host to an elite panel of judges. The events are likely to have a phenomenal impact on social media with the posts reaching around 50000 people. As a highlight this year, Recharge '20 will also host a Themed fashion walk event and a fun filled Dj Evening. Recharge '20 is a prodigious event that every artist and performer would look forward to!
