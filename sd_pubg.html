RULES

TOURNAMENT RULES:
1. Teams much register with 4 members (SQUAD) with no substitutes. Participants cannot register without a team.
2. Open teams are allowed.
3. Only one person per team must register.
4. Registration is limited to 200 participants i.e 50 teams.
5. Registration for students from Rajalakshmi Institutions’ will be opened at a later date, which will be intimated at our social media handles.
6. There will not be any participation fee for students from Rajalakshmi Institutions
7. Payment must be completed online via the link.
8. Participants must be present in the room while playing.
9. Players must bring their own mobiles for the tournament and no amenities for charging will be provided.
10. Room code will be provided at the venue.
11. Participants ID will be verified at the venue, and they must be present at the venue 30 minutes before the commencement of the tournament.
GAME RULES:
1. Emulators are strictly not allowed, and the team will be disqualified if found.
2. Modded APKs, Rooted devices are strictly prohibited.
3. Bugs and glitches may not be exploited to your advantage. If found, the team will be disqualified.
4. Misconduct during the tournament will disqualify the entire team.
5. Players must refrain from using offensive language.
6. Organizer’s decision will be final under any circumstances.

GENERAL RULES

1. Participants must bring their photo IDs.
2. Participants without photo ID cards will not be allowed to participate.
3. Verification will be done at the registration desk.
4. Students can participate in more than one event.
5. All the participants have to register online.
6. Registration fees must be paid online for all the events.
7. Last date for online registrations is March 7th.
8. Accommodation, if required must be intimated during registration.
9. Participants are requested to report to the venue half an hour before the event.
10. Students should strictly abide by the rules and regulations of the event.
